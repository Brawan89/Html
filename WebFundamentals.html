Some tips to add an image to your website

Some tips that will help you not to fall into illegal things and embarrassment when you want to add an image to your website:

  • Make sure you own the image.
  • If you do not own the image, make sure you have permission from the owner of the image.
  • Or the photo should be in the public domain.
